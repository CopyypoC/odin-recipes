Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish with rice noodles, broth, herbs, and meat. This recipe will use beef as is typically used, but chicken is also another popular option.

Ingredients

  • Beef Soup Bones
  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef Top Sirloin
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Star Anise
  • Salt
  • Garnishes like cilantro, green onion, Thai basil, lime, and anything else you would like.

Steps

  1. Roast the beef bones and onions until the bones are browned and the onions are soft.
  2. Make the broth with the bones and onions.
  3. Soak the rice noodles for an hour. Meanwhile, bring the broth to a simmer.
  4. Boil the noodles, then drain and add them to a bowl.
  5. Top with sirloin, cilantro, green onion, and broth. Garnish with your desired toppings.
